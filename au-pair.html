A Typical Workday

Your day starts at 8 AM. You care for the baby until 4 PM. This means feeding, diaper changes, nap time, hands-on play, daily outdoor time, and preparing homemade baby food following our written guidelines. At 4 PM, the baby comes back to us and your day is done. We are always nearby if you need anything.

Distractions

Do not watch videos or scroll social media while you are working. If the baby is sleeping, it is okay to change music or a podcast, but we ask that you limit phone use when working.

Vacation and Travel

We request one month's notice before you take your vacation time, but two weeks' notice is okay. If you travel with us on family trips, we pay for everything except your personal souvenirs.
