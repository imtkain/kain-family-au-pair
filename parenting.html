Our Parenting Approach

Parenting

We follow Montessori principles from birth. We have thought carefully about how we want our child to grow up, and we ask our au pair to share and consistently follow our approach. These values are the primary reason we chose an au pair over daycare.

Our Core Rules

Our Core Rules

Zero Screens Around the Baby

No TV, tablets, phones, or electronic toys. The au pair's own phone should not be used while caring for the baby. This is our most important rule.

Homemade Baby Food Only

No store-bought baby food. Written food guidelines will be provided and must be followed without daily reminders.

Calm, Structured Environment

The home is quiet and routine matters. The baby thrives on consistency.

Hands-On, Independent Play

Toys and activities are intentional. Daily outdoor time is part of every day.

Montessori Redirection

No yelling. Stay calm and redirect. As the baby grows, we discuss new situations together and stay consistent.

We trust you to follow these values even when we are not in the room. That trust is the foundation of this arrangement.

What Is Montessori?

What Is Montessori?

Montessori is an approach to early childhood learning that respects the child's natural development. It emphasizes hands-on exploration, independence, calm environments, and following the child's lead. For an infant, this means real toys instead of screens, safe spaces to move and explore, and a caregiver who observes and supports rather than directs.
